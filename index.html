Alternative Fuel Filling Stations

Why?

With electric cars becoming cheaper more ubiquitous the excuses for driving one with an internal combustion engine are dwindling. One major hurdle however, is availability of places to "fill up" your car. Driving 500 miles to see family doesn't work too well if your car has a 150 mile range and the only charging station is in your garage.

I got interested to look at electric charging stations, and before I knew it I had found a dataset with not just electric charging stations around the country, but all filling stations for alternative fuels. It seemed like a perfect opportunity to use the beautiful d3.hexbins() to gain insight into patterns from the 18k+ data-points.

How to use

  • Click on the legend to filter by type of renewable fuel.
  • Think of what might explain the trends in the data. For instance, what explains the distribution of corn-based ethanol stations?
  • Hover over the hexagons for a cool but useless effect.
